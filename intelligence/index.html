Your Current System Isn't Actually Smart. It's Just Scheduled.

Most HVAC systems sold as "smart" are timers with a phone app. They turn on at 6am because you told them to. They don't know your solar is generating, that today's warmer than yesterday, that your tariff spikes at 3pm, or that your family is home early.

They react. They don't anticipate.

Thermal Dawn is built around a different idea: your heating and cooling should make decisions for you, optimising across solar, weather, tariffs, and your home's thermal behaviour, without you having to think about it.

Four Things Our AI Optimises Simultaneously

Your Solar Generation

Thermal Dawn monitors your solar output in real time and forecasts tomorrow's generation from local weather. Charging happens when your panels are producing, not when the grid is expensive.

Your Energy Tariffs

We ingest your tariff schedule automatically, time-of-use rates, peak windows, off-peak valleys, feed-in rates, and charge thermal storage when electricity is cheapest.

Your Local Weather

Thermal Dawn pulls hyperlocal forecasts and adjusts charging depth accordingly, storing more ahead of a cold front, less on a mild day.

Your Home's Thermal Behaviour

Over time, Thermal Dawn builds a model of how your specific home holds and loses heat, making every decision more accurate each month.

Yes, natively, out of the box. Full local control, full sensor visibility, and full automation, with no community workarounds or Modbus adapters. There's also a complete open API for everything else.
