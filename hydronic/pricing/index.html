The Real Cost of Staying on Gas.

Gas hydronic heating costs the average Australian household $1,657–$1,814 per year in energy alone, according to Sustainability Victoria's 2024 data. Over 15 years that's over $27,000 just in running costs, before a single gas price increase.

Gas network charges have risen consistently for a decade and there's no credible scenario where gas gets cheaper. Every year you stay on gas locks in that trajectory.

Thermal Dawn shifts your hydronic heating load to solar energy you've already generated, dropping your annual thermal running costs to near zero for the system's lifetime. Most customers recoup their investment in three to five years, then run on free solar for the next fifteen.
